## About

A two-phase game insipred by **Defender (1981)**.

Phase 1 is a 2D side-scrolling rescue mission where the player saves mechanical bots from an alien swarm. until an unbeatable boss forces their death. The screen tears open into a fully 3D aerial arena where every bot rescued orbits the ship as a glowing drone. Those drones are a strong weapon capable of breaching enemy shields.

**The Twist (one sentence):**
> *"The mechanical survivors rescued in the 2D phase orbit your ship as drones in the 3D phase and can breach enemy shields with one hit."*

## How to Play

- **Phase 1 (2D):** Move with `WASD` / arrow keys. Rescue bots. Survive as long as possible.
- **Phase 2 (3D):** Fly with `WASD`. Aim with mouse. `Left_Mouse_Click` Fires a laser, `Space` launches a drone missile. Drain enemy shields, then gun them down.
